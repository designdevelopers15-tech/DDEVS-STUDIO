Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most logo and packaging projects are completed within 3-7 days, while mechanical designs may take 1-2 weeks depending on complexity. We provide a detailed timeline at project initiation.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! Our Basic plan includes 2 revisions, Standard includes 3, and Premium offers unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied with the result.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all standard file formats (JPG, PNG, PDF, AI for vectors) along with source files. For mechanical designs, we provide CAD files in your preferred format.

How do I start a project?

Simply click the "Order Now" button, fill out our project brief form, and we'll get back to you within 24 hours to discuss your requirements and provide a quote.
